☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis
📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data from a coffee shop to uncover actionable insights that can enhance business performance. The interactive dashboard visualizes key metrics such as total sales, footfall, average bill per person, sales by time and location, and product performance.

🎯 Objective
To identify trends and patterns in sales data that can help improve revenue, optimize store operations, and enhance customer satisfaction.

🔍 Key Insights
Total Sales: $698,812.33
Total Footfall: 149,116 customers
Average Bill per Person: $4.69
Average Orders per Person: 1.44
Peak Sales Hours: 8 AM – 10 AM
Top Location: Hell’s Kitchen
Top 5 Products: Barista Espresso, Brewed Chai Tea, Hot Chocolate, Gourmet Brewed Coffee, Brewed Black Tea
Highest Sales Category: Coffee (39% of revenue)

📅 Recommended Analyses
Sales trends by day of the week and hour of the day
Monthly revenue performance
Comparison across store locations
Average price/order per person metrics
Category-wise and product-wise sales distribution

🛠 Technologies Used
Power BI / Excel (Data Visualization)
Excel (Data Cleaning & Prep)
Data Analysis & Dashboard Design
